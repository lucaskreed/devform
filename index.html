Squid's Development Team

Join the team and help create something extraordinary!

  • UI Designer (6%): Designs user interfaces for a smooth player experience.
  • Scripters (10% total): Develop scripts to implement gameplay mechanics.
  • Sr. Builder / Modeler (5%): Lead environment and map creation.
  • Animator (4%): Develop animations for characters and objects.
  • Audio Producer (4%): Create sound effects and music for the game.
  • Artist (4%): Create concept art and visual assets.
  • Jr. Builders / Modelers (6% total): Assist with building tasks.
  • Studio Manager (1%): Oversee team operations and deadlines.
  • Media Team: Promote the game and manage community engagement (no payment).
  • Moderators: Paid separately.
  • Trainee Moderators: Paid separately.
  • Fan Map Designer: Collaborate on community-inspired maps (no payment).
  • Studio Director: Leads the team vision and direction (no payment).
