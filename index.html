Squid's Development Team

Join the team and help create something extraordinary!

  • Studio Director: Define and lead the team's vision and direction.
  • Studio Manager: Oversee team operations, workflows, and deadlines.
  • Sr. Builder / Modeler: Lead the creation of environments and maps.
  • Scripters: Develop and implement scripts for gameplay mechanics.
  • Animator: Create animations for characters, objects, and events.
  • Audio Producer: Develop sound effects and music to enhance the game.
  • Artist: Design concept art and create visual assets.
  • UI Designer: Designs intuitive user interfaces for a seamless player experience.
  • Jr. Builders / Modelers: Assist with building and modeling tasks.
  • Media Team: Promote the game and manage community engagement.
  • Events Team: Plan and execute events to engage the community.
  • Moderators: Monitor and manage community interactions.
  • Trainee Moderators: Assist moderators while learning the role.
  • Fan Map Designer: Collaborate with the team to create community-inspired maps.
  • Beta Tester: Participate in testing new features and providing feedback.

Profits are distributed as follows:

General Allocations

  • Group Funds: 40%
  • Advertisements: 20%
  • Member Payouts: 40%
