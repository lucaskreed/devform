Spilled Ink Studios Staff Team Application

Join the team and help create something extraordinary!

  • Founder & Head of Studio / CEO: Oversees the overall vision and direction of the studio.
  • Studio Executive Director: Leads and directs studio operations and strategy.
  • Creative Director Executive: Oversees creative decisions and vision for all studio projects.
  • Community Engagement Executive: Manages community relations and engagement strategies.
  • Developer Outreach Executive: Coordinates partnerships with developers and handles outreach.
  • Game Development Lead: Leads game development teams and ensures projects meet deadlines.
  • Development Lead: Oversees the technical and development aspects of the studio's projects.
  • Creative Lead: Leads the creative and design teams for visual, audio, and UI/UX work.
  • Community Manager: Manages the community, ensuring members have a positive experience.
  • Community Events Lead: Leads the planning and execution of community events.
  • Server Events Lead: Oversees server events and ensures smooth execution.
  • Marketing Specialist Lead: Manages the marketing team and drives studio promotions.
  • Support Specialist Lead: Leads the support team and handles inquiries and issues.
  • Developer Outreach Lead: Manages developer relations and collaborations.
  • Game Reviewer: Reviews and evaluates games submitted for features or reviews.
  • Developer Relations Specialist: Acts as a liaison between the studio and developers.
  • Help Desk Agent: Provides support to the community and helps resolve issues.
  • Technical Support Agent: Assists with technical issues within the community or games.
  • Social Media Manager: Handles the studio's social media presence and content.
  • Content Strategist: Develops content strategies for promotion and engagement.
  • Advertising Coordinator: Plans and manages advertising campaigns for the studio.
  • PR Specialist: Manages public relations and communications for the studio.
  • Builder/Modeler: Builds and models 3D assets and environments for games.
  • Animator: Creates animations for characters, objects, and events.
  • Scripter: Writes code and scripts for gameplay mechanics and systems.
  • UI/UX Designer: Designs and improves user interfaces and user experiences.
  • Bot Developer: Develops bots and automation tools for the community and studio use.
  • Software Engineer: Works on software development for the studio's tools and services.
  • Graphic Designer: Designs visual assets for branding and promotional material.
  • Artist: Creates concept art and visual designs for the studio's projects.
  • Video Editor: Edits and produces video content for promotional purposes.
  • Audio Producer: Creates sound effects, music, and voiceovers for games and media.
  • Moderator: Monitors and manages community interactions to ensure a positive environment.
  • Sr. Moderator: Senior-level moderators who oversee other moderators and ensure smooth operations.
  • Forums Moderator: Oversees forums and maintains order and engagement within the forums.
  • Community Event Coordinator: Plans and coordinates community events and activities.
  • Community Event Host: Hosts and manages community events to ensure smooth execution.
  • Server Event Coordinator: Plans and coordinates events hosted directly within the server.
  • Server Event Host: Hosts server-based events and activities for the community.

Profits are distributed as follows:

General Allocations

  • Group Funds: 40%
  • Advertisements: 20%
  • Member Payouts: 40%
